Welcome to Bunjay Curry

Welcome to Bunjay Curry, a curry-based Caribbean spot that brings bold flavors and comfort to every plate. Our menu is built around rich spices, warm seasonings, and dishes that feel like home — even if it’s your first time trying Caribbean food. We’re excited to share a taste of the islands with you. Come by our restaurant and escape to the West Indies where you can immerse yourself in the colors and tastes of the islands. Our food is inspired by countries like Barbados, Trinidad, and Guyana. Jump outside of your normal tastes and try something new. We promise you'll love it.

About Us

Bunjay Curry started because I fell in love with Caribbean food while living in the West Indies. The strong, spicy flavors, the heavy use of spices, and the blend of African and Indian influences make Caribbean curries truly unique. They’re unlike anything else — deep, rich, and full of history. After experiencing those flavors firsthand, I wanted to bring that same energy and authenticity back home. Bunjay Curry is my way of sharing the food that inspired me and the culture that shaped it. Try our fresh fired egg-balls with mango-sour sauce.
