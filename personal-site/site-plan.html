Overview

Purpose

The goal of this small webpage is to introduce a fictional restaurant with pictures and description for the customers. Then the child page will be a menu with options for the customer to see with pictures of the dishes as well as prices.

Audience

The customers who are interested in trying our delicious curries and Caribbean delights. It also will be the homepage and menu for getting customers to the restaurant.

Dynamic elements

On the menu page, users can filter menu items by category (Breakfast / Lunch / Dinner / Desserts) AND click “Add to Order” buttons that update a running total price.

Wireframes

Create two wireframes for your site. One for each page and list them here

Home

The first page will be fairly simple. It will have the information about the restaurant and its origins and location. It will show and image of the restaurant front. It will have a couple pictures of the food and a button at the bottom that leads to the menu page. The menu button at the top will so the same thing.

Menu

This page will have the same buttons at the top. The home button will take you back to the welcome page. There will be a description of the foods origins and inspiration. There will then be a list of all the menu items with descriptions and prices. There will be an add to order button next to each item. A picture will be next to each dish. At the bottom of the page will be a price totaling section that will be the dynamic java part of the page. With each item you add it will change the total of their order. At the top of the page you will be able to there will be a spot where you can filter through the types of food and dishes.
